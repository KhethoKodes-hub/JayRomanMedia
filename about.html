Introduction

Jay Roman
Media

Jay B Roman Media is a visual content creation company based in Kathu, founded in 2016 by John Roman. We specialise in corporate, lifestyle, and maternity photography.

Unlike traditional photographers who merely capture images, we create media solutions tailored to help individuals and companies present themselves powerfully and professionally.

"We aim to uplift communities by creating jobs while delivering high-quality, fast-turnaround visual content that makes our clients unforgettable."

— John Roman, Founder
